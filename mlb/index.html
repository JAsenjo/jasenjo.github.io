About the app

AI Sports: MLB is a dedicated Major League Baseball app that combines standings, schedule, rich game details, and AI-driven analysis. You get a clear read on each matchup: written AI preview, implied win odds from lines, and—when available—estimated win chance, totals, and consensus-style summaries.

The app is more than a scoreboard: team and player context, injuries, news, head-to-head when it matters, and AI content published ahead of first pitch so you can follow the league with better context.

What you get

  • 📊 Standings & schedule — Browse the MLB schedule by day and open any game for status, lines, and detail. Follow how teams stack up as the season moves.
  • 🤖 AI match insights — AI Preview and implied odds are available broadly; deeper forecasts unlock with VIP or, where offered, by watching a rewarded ad per game. Final games may show full insight without a paywall.
  • 📋 Game detail — Preview and summary tabs, stats comparisons, pitching and matchup context, location and venue, and more—aligned with how you follow baseball on mobile.
  • 🏥 Injuries & news — Injury reports and league news to stay current off the field.
  • ⭐ VIP — Ad-free experience on supported screens and full access to premium AI content. Manage your subscription in your device settings (App Store or Google Play).

About the app

Efemérides is a daily discovery app with historical events, births, deaths, holidays, cultural curiosities and trivia. The experience is designed for reading: clean cards, easy navigation, saved favorites and a focus on content you can browse quickly each day.

We combine editorial content with rich detail screens, so each item can be opened for more context, related facts and supporting imagery when available.

What you get

  • 🗓️ Today — Daily events, births, deaths and holidays with a clear reading-first layout.
  • 🎨 Culture — Curated culture cards with multilingual content and detail views.
  • ❓ Trivia — A daily quiz with timed completion and a friendly ranking screen.
  • ⭐ Favorites — Save ephemerides, culture items and trivia-related content for later.
  • ⚙️ Settings — Language and theme controls tuned for a comfortable reading experience.

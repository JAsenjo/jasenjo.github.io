Privacy Policy

Hoy en la Historia

Hoy en la Historia is a daily history and culture app. This policy explains how we handle information when you use the app on iOS or Android. It is written to align with common app store privacy requirements and applicable data protection laws.

1. Data controller

The app is operated by the Hoy en la Historia team. For privacy-related requests, contact us at hoyenlahistoria@gmail.com (please use “Privacy” in the subject when possible).

2. Information we collect

We collect only what is needed to run the app, deliver content, and improve quality:

  • Device and usage data: App interactions, device model, operating system version, and crash reports to improve stability and performance.
  • Preferences stored on device: Language, theme, favorites, and local cache are saved on your device so the app can work smoothly.
  • Notification token: If you allow notifications, we may store a push token to deliver reminders or updates.
  • Advertising identifiers: If ads are used, platform advertising identifiers may be processed for measurement or personalization when allowed by the operating system and your consent choices.

3. How we use your information

We use the information above to provide the app, remember your settings, keep favorites available, show relevant content, measure performance, and improve the service. We do not sell your personal data.

4. Third-party services

Depending on the version of the app, we may rely on services such as analytics, crash reporting, cloud storage, image sources, or app store purchase systems. Each service has its own privacy policy and terms.

5. Data retention

We retain data only as long as needed for the purposes described above or as required by law. You may request deletion of your data by contacting us.

6. Your rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, or delete your data, and to object to certain processing. To exercise these rights, contact us at the email below.

7. Contact

For privacy questions or data requests, contact us at hoyenlahistoria@gmail.com. Please include “Privacy” in the subject line when possible.

8. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The latest version will always be published on this page.
